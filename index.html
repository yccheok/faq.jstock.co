  • Watchlist

  • How to delete a stock

    Long press on the stock. A trash icon will shown on top right. Click on the trash icon.

  • How to display stock code

    Long press on "Name" table header. A pop-up will shown. Choose "Code".

  • Can't find XYZ stock

    Type in full Yahoo stock code format. For instance, the Yahoo stock code for Singapore DBS Group Holdings Ltd is "D05.SI".

  • Premium

  • Can't find previous purchased Premium

    If you can't find your previously purchased Premium, make sure you're signed in to the same account as when you made your purchase. Please refer to https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/113410?hl=en.

  • Is Premium one-time purchase

    Yes.
